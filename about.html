About Us

Experience

Felice's past work experience includes: Controller for a large mental health organization (1 year) Manager of the Bookkeeping Division for an amazing accounting firm where she started as the sole bookkeeper. From there, she built the department to 5 employees where she managed both 50+ clients and all employees. (3 years) Accounting Manager for McMaster University Association of Part-Time Students (4 years) As well as several other bookkeeping and accounting jobs since she began her career in 2010

Education

Felice's education is unique in relation to her fellow bookkeepers. She was in the CGA program (program to become an accountant) when she had her son. Though she didn't finish the program due to motherhood, her level of education is much higher than the average bookkeeper, allowing her to understand and facilitate higher level accounting theory practices. She graduated from McMaster University with an advanced accounting diploma and from Mohawk College with a diploma in Accounting, both of which she graduated with honors and at the top of her class
